## The Network Setup

Deasil Networks runs a BGP network that's directly connected or one hop away from all major carriers. In plain terms: your latency stays low because they're not bouncing your traffic around before it hits the internet. For anything that's latency-sensitive — e-commerce checkouts, media streaming, real-time apps — this matters more than people realize.
